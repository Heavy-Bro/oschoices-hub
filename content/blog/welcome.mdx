# Welcome to OSChoices

Every day you face small decisions that eat up mental energy: what to eat, how to budget, which option to pick. None of these are hard in isolation — they're just friction. OSChoices exists to remove that friction.

## What Is OSChoices?

OSChoices is a portfolio of small, focused web tools. Each app does one thing well. No accounts required, no paywalls, no bloat — just the tool you need, fast.

The name says it all: **OS** stands for *Open* and *Simple*. Every tool we build should be open to use and simple to understand in under 30 seconds.

## What's Coming

We're building tools across a few categories:

- **Utility** — quick-decision helpers for daily life
- **Finance** — simple calculators and trackers
- **Productivity** — focus tools without the subscription tax
- **Creator** — lightweight tools for makers and writers

The first app is launching soon. Subscribe below to be the first to know.

## Why Indie Tools?

The best tools are often the smallest ones. A focused tool respects your time — it doesn't upsell you, doesn't ask for your data, and doesn't bury the feature you need behind three menus.

That's the standard we're holding ourselves to. If an app can't explain itself in one sentence, we rebuild it until it can.

Stay tuned. There's a lot in the pipeline.

— The OSChoices Team
